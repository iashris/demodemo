PLANNIO

Architectural planning is an abstract process. It happens through a series of iterations, some driven by logic and some by instincts. Traditional CAD tools like AutoCAD are Revit are useful for representing a precise design. However most of the non-precise, or the abstract part is done by the architect on pen and paper.
Plannio is designed to be a nifty tool to be used by an architect for quick ideation before the plannig process. The computer doesn't just help you represent the design but also participates in the design process by offering you hints and guides.
